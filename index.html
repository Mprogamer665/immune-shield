Project Overview

COVID-19 is caused by the virus SARS-CoV-2. This project explains how the virus spreads, how the immune system responds, and why prevention strategies like vaccination, ventilation, and hygiene protect individuals and communities.

Key Question: How can scientific understanding of COVID-19 help us make responsible choices that protect ourselves and others?

1) COVID-19 & Virus Science

  • COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by SARS-CoV-2.
  • SARS-CoV-2 is an RNA virus with a lipid envelope and spike proteins.
  • It spreads mainly through respiratory particles, especially indoors.
  • Spike proteins bind to receptors on human cells, allowing infection.

2) Immune System Response

Innate immunity (fast)

First-response defenses act quickly but non-specifically.

Adaptive immunity (specific)

B cells produce antibodies. T cells help destroy infected cells and build long-term memory.

Vaccination trains this adaptive response without causing COVID-19 disease.

3) Prevention & Public Health

✅ Vaccination and boosters
✅ Better indoor ventilation
✅ Handwashing and respiratory hygiene
✅ Staying home when unwell
✅ Masking in high-risk settings
✅ Protecting vulnerable people

Families: Prevention protects high-risk loved ones.
